Cheetahs

Fun facts about cheetahs

  • Cheetahs are the fastest land animals in the world, reaching speeds of up to 75 miles per hour.
  • Cheetahs have long, slender bodies and slender legs.
  • Cheetahs have spotted fur and a black tear mark running down each cheek.
  • Cheetahs are carnivores and eat a variety of animals, including gazelles, zebras, and wildebeest.

Information about cheetahs

Cheetahs are an endangered species and their population is declining in the wild. There are a number of factors that contribute to the cheetah's decline, including habitat loss, poaching, and conflict with humans.
